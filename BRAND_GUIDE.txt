# Brand Guide - Sonic Voyager

## Typography
- **Headings**: 'Orbitron', sans-serif (Bold, Uppercase, Futuristic)
- **Body**: 'Rajdhani', sans-serif (Clean, Technical)
- **Monospace**: 'Share Tech Mono', monospace (For data/numbers)

## Colors
- **Background**: #000000 (Void Black)
- **Surface**: rgba(10, 10, 10, 0.8) (Deep Space Glass)
- **Primary Text**: #FFFFFF (Starlight White)
- **Secondary Text**: #888888 (Titanium Grey)
- **Accent Primary**: #FFFFFF (Pure White - for high contrast active states)
- **Accent Secondary**: #333333 (Carbon Fiber - for subtle borders)

## Components
- **Buttons**:
  - Default: Transparent with 1px solid rgba(255,255,255,0.2)
  - Hover: rgba(255,255,255,0.1) background
  - Active: White background, Black text
  - Record Button:
    - Default: Transparent with border, white text
    - Recording State: rgba(255,0,0,0.1) background, red border (#ff0000), red text
    - Icon: Pulsing animation when recording
- **Cards (Bento)**:
  - Background: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.7)
  - Backdrop Filter: blur(20px)
  - Border: 1px solid rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.1)
  - Radius: 4px (Sharp, technical corners)

## Icons
- Style: Minimalist, Stroke-based, White. No fill unless active.
- Policy: No emojis. Use geometric shapes, text symbols (e.g. >>), or SVG icons.
